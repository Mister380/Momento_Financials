Executive Overview

Momento is designed to simplify how busy professionals, cultural enthusiasts, and social groups discover, schedule, and book cultural events.

This financial model demonstrates the economic viability of the Momento project over a 5-year period with a bootstrap approach, focusing on self-funding, sustainable growth, and profitability.

Recommendations

1

Bootstrap Strategy

Launch with minimal initial investment of €50,000, focusing on essential features and early revenue generation to fund future development organically.

2

Sustainable Growth

Adopt a measured approach to user acquisition, prioritizing quality over quantity and targeting the highest-value segments first to optimize resources.

3

Strategic Partnerships

Form revenue-sharing partnerships with event organizers to reduce upfront costs while ensuring a strong supply of quality events on the platform.

4

Feature Prioritization

Focus development resources on features that directly generate revenue and improve retention, particularly the calendar integration and recommendation algorithm.

5

Early Monetization

Introduce premium features within the first 6 months to begin generating subscription revenue earlier, testing different price points with early adopters.

6

Cost Control

Maintain strict cost control by leveraging freelancers and contractors instead of full-time employees initially, and using affordable cloud solutions to minimize infrastructure costs.

7

Metrics-Driven Scaling

Use detailed analytics to identify when key metrics justify additional investment, scaling resources in direct proportion to validated revenue growth.
